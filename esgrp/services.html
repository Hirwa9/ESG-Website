    ESG Services

    At ESG, we take joy in sharing blessings with the wider community. Through our singing, we provide various spiritual services. We are committed to ensuring that your events unfold exactly as you envision, if not better. And so, you can relax and allow us to make it happen for you.

    All our services are based on religion and often involve celebrating the Holy Mass.

    • Regular Holy Mass
    • Sacrements Holy Mass
    • Wedding Holy Mass
    • Religious concerts
    • Requiem Mass

    Regular Holy Mass

    Normally, we hold Holy Mass celebrations on various days, and should you ever wish for us to sing at one of your special occasions, please don't hesitate to reach out.

    Sacrements Holy Mass

    A sacrament is a religious ceremony that imparts divine grace. The Church recognizes seven sacraments instituted by the Lord: Baptism, Confirmation, Eucharist, Penance, Anointing of the Sick, Marriage, and Holy Orders. During Sacred Holy Mass, the Holy Father grants new gifts to transform the world and thus these all those ceremonies are sacred and require a peaceful environment. ESG takes pleasure in accompanying and singing for the Christian community during Holy Mass celebrations of those unforgettable events.

    Wedding Holy Mass

    There are countless unforgettable moments that people experience in life. Among the most significant is the decision to marry - the wedding! This occasion should be incredibly special for everyone involved, particularly for the bride and groom. To ensure your wishes come true, consider inviting us. With our presence, your wedding Holy Mass will become a prayerful memory for a lifetime, cherished not only by you but also by your family. Contact us

    Religious Concerts

    Religious concerts bring peace to our souls, especially during holidays like Christmas and Easter, celebrating Jesus Christ's birth and resurrection, and reminding us of God's love for His people. At ESG, we believe in praising the Lord at any time, not just on holidays, and we enjoy sharing the joy of praising Him through music. By expanding beyond holidays, religious groups can foster unity, creativity, and continuous worship year-round, which also engages a wider audience and promotes interfaith dialogue, spreading the message of faith and community.

    Requiem Mass

    Unfortunately, we may lose our beloved ones when we need them most. In such moments, we should not miss the opportunity to lay their souls to rest in peace. Praying for our departed family and friends is an act of love, remembrance, and spiritual connection. Join us to find comfort through celebrating a Requiem Mass.
