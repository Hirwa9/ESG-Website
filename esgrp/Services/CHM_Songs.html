      Upload a song

      If you have any composition that you would like to share, this is where to start. - How to - Upload the composition's file and we will include it into the collection. It requires only a few clicks.

      We aim to facilitate access to widely used songs in the Catholic Church for various musicians and choirs. When you contribute a song here, it becomes part of a shared list accessible to everyone. This ensures easy and cost-effective discovery from a centralized location.

      Note

      Before uploading a song, please ensure the following:

      • Confirm that the song is not already included in our shared collection.
      • Provide a clear music sheet in PDF format.
      Unclear files (copies) will be rejected upon review.
