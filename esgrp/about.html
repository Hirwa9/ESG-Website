    Eastern Singers Group

    Welcome to the online home of our cherished religious group. A harmonious congregation of individuals brought together by faith and a shared passion for musical worship. Through the power of song we seek to inspire, uplift and connect with the divine and with one another.

    Join us on this journey as we raise our voices in praise, cerebrating the unifying force of spirituality and music. Explore our website to discover more about mission, repertoire, and upcoming events.

    About us

    Origin and membership

    Eastern Singers Group is a group of Rwandan Catholic singers. Surprisingly, our group members are not only from the East as someone can think, but also members who are already singers in other choirs from different parts of the country.

    Background

    Due to our members' roots and the abundance, our group is proudly designated as ESG (Eastern Singers Group), representing a choir-like ensemble. We could potentially amass a remarkable number of participants, exceeding two to three hundred singers if joinned forces with our affiliated choirs.

    What we do

    ESG is a religious singing group. We sing and help people to stay close to the Lord by offering different services.
    All our services are religious and mostly goes with celebrating the Holy Mass.

    Location

    Our main basement is Rutonde Parish - Kigali Archdiocese, and the group singers are from other different choirs most of which operates in the Eastern and Northern Provinces of Rwanda and Kigali City.

    Identity

    Our logo

    Our logo stands as a captivating emblem of our identity, effortlessly encapsulating the core values and vision of ESG in a single, artful design. It is a two-blocks figure whose ESG abbreviation lies on the top, preceeding with a musical note as it holds the first 'E' letter of the abbreviation.
    The next block contains the words "Eastern Singers Group" as a full explaination of ESG abbreviation.

    Soprano

    A term used in music to describe the highest vocal range for female singers. Soprano singers typically have the highest-pitched voices in choral and operatic music. They can sing notes in the higher register and often have the ability to hit very high and piercing notes with clarity and power.

    Read more

    Alto

    An alto voice is a vocal range in music typically designated for female singers. It is the second-highest voice type among female singers. Female singers with alto voices typically sing in the alto range, which spans from approximately G3 (the G below middle C) to E5 (the E above middle C), although the specific range can vary depending on the individual singer's vocal ability.

    Read more

    Tenor

    A tenor voice is a high male vocal range in music. It is one of the main voice types for male singers, and tenors typically sing in the higher-pitched male vocal range. The tenor range typically spans from approximately C3 (the C below middle C) to C5 (the C above middle C), but it can vary depending on the individual singer's vocal range and abilities.

    Read more

    Bass

    A bass voice is a low male vocal range in music, and it is one of the main voice types for male singers. Bass singers typically sing in the lower-pitched male vocal range, known for its deep and resonant qualities. The bass voice range usually spans from approximately E2 (the E below middle C) to E4 (the E above middle C), but the specific range can vary depending on the individual singer's vocal abilities.
